Tools

Our project was made using the following tools :
Godot 4.0 as our game engine of choice for its open source nature and its ease of use.
Modern Interiors by LimeZu for visuals, bar some customs assets and animations.
Pixabay for royalty free sound effects.
La Danse Macabre from Jake Kaufman for our main theme, this music was sourced from the Shovel Knight OST
